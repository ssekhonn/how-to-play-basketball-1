Objective:

The primary goal of basketball is to score points by shooting the ball through the opponent's hoop.

Game Duration:

Games typically consist of four quarters, each lasting 10-12 minutes, depending on the level of play.

Teams:

Each game features two teams of five players each, striving to outscore their opponents.

Court Layout:

The court is divided into two main sections by the mid-court line. Each team defends their hoop while attempting to score in their opponent's hoop.

Basic Rules:

  • A game begins with a tip-off at center court.
  • Players dribble the ball by bouncing it on the ground.
  • Points are scored through field goals (2 or 3 points) and free throws (1 point).
